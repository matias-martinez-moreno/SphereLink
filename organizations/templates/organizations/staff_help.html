Your Staff Powers

Quick Actions
Create Event
  1. Click "Create Event" in sidebar
  2. Fill event details (title, date, location, capacity)
  3. Choose event type (Sports, Wellness, Academic, Other)
  4. Your event is automatically marked as Official
Moderate Content
  1. View any event in your organization
  2. Click to delete inappropriate content
  3. Delete comments that violate community guidelines
  4. Keep your organization's events clean and professional
Key Features
What You Can Do
  • Create any type of event (same as members)
  • All events automatically get "Official" badge
  • Delete any event or comment
  • View and manage event registrations
Important Notes
  • Your events are always Official (no option to change)
  • You can edit/delete any content in your organization
  • Use moderation powers responsibly
