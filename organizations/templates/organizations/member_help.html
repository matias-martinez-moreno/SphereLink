Quick Actions
Create Event
  1. Click "Create Event" in sidebar
  2. Fill event details (title, description, date, location)
  3. Choose type: Community (public) or Organization (private)
  4. Set maximum capacity and click "Create Event"
Manage Profile
  1. Click "My Profile" in sidebar
  2. Click "Edit Profile" to update information
  3. Upload a profile photo
  4. View your event statistics and notifications
Event Types
Community Events

Open to all members. Anyone in your organization can see and join.

Organization Events

Private to your organization. Only members of your organization can see and join.

Note: You cannot create Official events. Only Staff members can create Official events with special badges.
